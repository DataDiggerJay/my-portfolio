About Me

I’m Jayesh Berde, a B.E. Computer Science Engineering (Data Science) graduate with an exceptional CGPA of 9.42. Currently, I work as a Growth Hacker and Data Analyst at Feedspot, where I transform complex datasets into actionable insights. I also have hands-on experience integrating ChatGPT’s Prompt API, Spotify API, and iTunes API into real-world data solutions. With a passion for learning and innovation, I enjoy building tools—from sleek frontends to intelligent backend solutions—that solve real problems. My goal is to grow as a full-stack, data-driven developer and contribute to impactful, innovative teams where technology and insight drive success.
